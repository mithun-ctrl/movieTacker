Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 21, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Cinedairy ("we," "our," or "us"). Cinedairy is a personal movie tracking application designed to help you log and rate movies you have watched in theatres. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

By accessing or using our mobile application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect specific types of information to provide and improve our Service.

A. Personal Information

When you create an account via Clerk (our authentication provider), we collect:

  • Email Address (Required): Used for authentication via One-Time Password (OTP) and account recovery.
  • First Name and Last Name (Optional): Used to personalize your profile.
  • Profile Image (Optional): Used to display a user avatar within the app.

B. User-Generated Content

We store the data you voluntarily input into the app, including:

  • Movie logs (titles of movies watched).
  • Watch dates.
  • Star ratings.
  • Personal notes or reviews attached to movie logs.

C. Device and Usage Data

We may collect standard information about your mobile device, such as your device model, operating system version, and IP address, to ensure app compatibility and security.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To verify your identity using Clerk’s email-based OTP system.
  • Service Provision: To store your movie logs and sync them across your devices.
  • Personalization: To display your optional profile photo and name.
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries or support requests.

4. Authentication and Security

We use Clerk as our third-party authentication provider.

  • Sign Up/Login: Cinedairy utilizes a passwordless flow. You log in by entering your email and verifying it via a One-Time Password (OTP) sent to your inbox.
  • Security: Your authentication credentials are managed securely by Clerk. Cinedairy does not store passwords. While we implement industry-standard security measures to protect your user content, please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We do not share your data with third-party advertising networks.

We only share data with service providers strictly necessary to run the app:

  • Clerk: For identity management and authentication.
  • Database/Hosting Providers: To physically store your movie logs and account details.

6. Children’s Privacy

Cinedairy is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will immediately delete this from our servers.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information and movie logs only for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, your data will be permanently removed from our active databases.
